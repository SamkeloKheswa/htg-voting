About Us

Umkhumbi Wamalokishi Movement is a community organisation dedicated to promoting local talent in Hammarsdale. Our mission is to provide a platform for young artists, performers, and creatives to showcase their skills and gain recognition through community events such as the Hammarsdale Got Talent competition.

The HGT Voting portal allows the public to participate in live voting for their favourite contestants, ensuring a fair and transparent process. Votes are counted in real-time, and the results are displayed live on the dashboard.
